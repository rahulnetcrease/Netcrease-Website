Challenge

The legacy monolith could not keep up with increased load, deployments were slow and risky, and the monitoring surface was fragmented across teams.

Solution

We designed a domain-driven, service-oriented architecture with clear service boundaries, implemented horizontal autoscaling, adopted containerization, and standardized CI/CD pipelines with automated tests and Canary releases.

Implementation Highlights

  • Refactored core modules into independently deployable services
  • Introduced centralized logging and distributed tracing (OpenTelemetry)
  • Implemented autoscaling groups and redundant failover paths
  • Automated blue/green deployments to reduce risk

Business Impact

Following the rollout, the client saw marked improvements in reliability, a measurable reduction in incident MTTR, and the ability to onboard new customers without major infra investments.
