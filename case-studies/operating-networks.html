Challenge

The client operated fragmented regional teams with inconsistent tooling, leading to frequent handoff errors and missed SLAs.

Solution

We standardized operating procedures, implemented a unified monitoring layer, created runbooks, and set up cross-region shift overlaps to ensure smooth handoffs.

Implementation Highlights

  • Unified real-time dashboards and alerts
  • Cross-training and shared runbooks
  • Automated incident routing with escalation policies

Business Impact

Operations became more predictable, MTTR dropped significantly, and the client maintained SLAs even during peak loads.
